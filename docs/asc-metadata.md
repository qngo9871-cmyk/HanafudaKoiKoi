## Description
Hanafuda Koi-Koi Go-Stop brings the classic East Asian flower card game to your phone —
known as Koi-Koi in Japan and Go-Stop in Korea, played with the same 48-card Hwatu deck.

MATCH THE MONTHS
Twelve months, four cards each. Match a card from your hand to a card on the field by
its month to capture both — then draw from the deck and match again. Simple to learn,
deep to master.

SCORE REAL YAKU
Chase authentic scoring combinations: Five Brights, Three Brights, Boar-Deer-Butterfly,
Poetry Ribbons, Blue Ribbons, Moon Viewing, Flower Viewing, and more. Every yaku from
the traditional rules is scored correctly.

KOI-KOI OR SHOUBU
Complete a yaku and you get to choose: call Koi-Koi to keep playing for a bigger score,
or call Shoubu to bank your points and end the hand. Push too far and your opponent
might catch up — that tension is the whole game.

PLAY YOUR WAY
- Three AI difficulty levels: Easy, Normal, Hard
- Best-of-three matches with running scores
- Clean, fast, no ads, no gambling, no real-money wagers — just the game
- Works completely offline

Whether you grew up calling it Koi-Koi, Go-Stop, or Hwatu, this is the same beautiful
game your family plays at New Year's — now on your phone, anytime.
